Welcome to Bangaru Telangana Trust

Bangaru Telangana Trust is a self funded charity for the last 9 years and with our own seva & will, we wanted to serve in our capacity. Recently, we have started fundraising to help more people and reach every district of Telangana. We want to serve as many people in our state as we could to make it a 'Bangaru Telangana'.

WE DO SEVA FOR Old age people ,differently abled people,orphanage people ,and roadside people .we help them and will make them beautiful with our work
